About Us

As you might expect of a company that began as a high-end auto technicians.

A performance parts store by Grid7. We are professionals in remapping vehicles’ Electronic Control Unit for an output that you desire. Earlier Carburetors were equipment that was used for mixing air and fuel in certain ratios to feed engine for combustion. But these devices are now largely replaced with fuel injection systems due to lesser efficiency and scalability of carburettors. The actions and incidents within an engine is now controlled by a microprocessor equipped electronic device, Engine Control Unit or ECU. The programming in these ECU differs vehicle by vehicle. And the job of every ECU is to receive the signal from different sensors within and sometimes outside engine. The software within ECU compares this signals received with the program or Map stored within it. Vehicle manufacturers program these ECU optimally because of many reasons. Remapping is technique by which this program is altered or sometimes removed and replaced with new programs according to one’s need which may be power, efficiency or something else. Dealing with this maps are so crucial that any mishandling may cause the vehicle with malfunctioning sometimes even refuse to start. And RedbandRacing is the one company with theoretical and practical experts and experienced professionals for a careful and professional approach on remapping a vehicle with high precision and best available tools.
